Replacing My Personal Logo

An unnecessary update ♦ 15 July, 2021

Back in early-autumn 2019, I experimented with 8-bit pixel art. I spent a few weeks drawing this way, but I found using GIMP was too time-dense. As much as I enjoyed the medium, I cannot bring myself to resume.

As to why I chose to change my logo? It kist felt like time. I stumbled upon CSS art on a social software development website. In all likelihood, I must have seen some in the past. Regardless, my new edition of my logo was drawn using pure CSS (and some HTML, of course, the divs), it was my actual first attempt drawing anything original. I'm mighty proud of my results.


The Pro's

The following, imho, is the benefits of the updated version of my logo:

  • more modern feel
  • made 100% with code
  • looks better on a larger surface
